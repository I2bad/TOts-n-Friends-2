# Design System Overview

This document outlines the core visual style and components of our product, ensuring consistency and brand identity across all user interfaces.

## 🎨 Color Palette

Our color palette is carefully selected to evoke the desired brand emotions and provide a clear visual hierarchy.

*   **Primary Color:** `#1E39DF` - This vibrant blue is the cornerstone of our brand, used for key interactive elements, calls to action, and primary branding.
*   **Secondary Color:** `#FBD940` - A bright yellow offering a dynamic contrast, suitable for secondary actions, highlights, and status indicators.
*   **Tertiary Color:** `#FAD1D6` - An additional accent color for highlights, badges, or decorative elements.
*   **Neutral Color:** `#d3d3d3` - A deep, versatile neutral for backgrounds, text, and other foundational UI elements.

## 🔠 Typography

Our typography system ensures readability and reinforces brand personality.

*   **Headline Font:** `Epilogue` - Used for all major headings and titles, providing a distinct and impactful presence.
*   **Body Font:** `Plus Jakarta Sans` - Chosen for its legibility and modern aesthetic, it's used for all paragraph text, descriptions, and longer content.
*   **Label Font:** `Plus Jakarta Sans` - Applied to form labels, buttons, and other interactive text elements, ensuring consistency with body text for clarity.

## 📐 Roundedness

Our UI elements feature `maximum, pill-shaped` roundedness, providing a soft, approachable, and modern aesthetic.

## ↔️ Spacing

We implement `normal` spacing, striking a balance between information density and visual comfort, creating an organized and user-friendly layout.
